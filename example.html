example 1 : sum of 2 no.

    ; lisp program to find sum of two no.

    (setq x 10)
    (setq y 20)


    (print (+ x y))
output : 
    30


example 2 : sub of 2 no.

download 
    ; program to find sub of two no.

    (defvar x 10)
    (setq y 20)

    (write (- x y))
output : 
    -10


Global variables are generally declared using the defvar construct.
